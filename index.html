// the maker

I photograph quiet things, then push them until they glow.

I'm N1GHT CHXN9. I keep returning to the same quiet things: a bare branch against a pale sky, a hillside losing itself in cloud, the last few minutes of light. I'm not trying to document any of it, only to keep the feeling a scene gave me before it drains away.

So I push each frame past where it wants to stop, until the color and contrast say what the moment felt like, not just what it looked like. Honestly, most of the work is me arguing with myself about how far is too far. This archive is where I keep having that argument, out loud.
